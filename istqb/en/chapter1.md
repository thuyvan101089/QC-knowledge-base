
# 🧠 Chapter 1: Fundamentals of Testing

## 🎯 What is Testing?
Testing is the process of evaluating a system to:
- Detect defects
- Ensure quality
- Reduce risks

---

## 🔥 7 Principles of Testing

1. Testing shows presence of defects  
2. Exhaustive testing is impossible  
3. Early testing  
4. Defect clustering  
5. Pesticide paradox  
6. Context dependent  
7. Absence of errors fallacy  

---

## 🔄 Verification vs Validation

- Verification → Are we building the product right?  
- Validation → Are we building the right product?  

---

## ⚠️ Error vs Defect vs Failure

- Error → human mistake  
- Defect → bug in code  
- Failure → system behaves incorrectly  

---

## 💡 Interview Tip
Testing is not only about finding bugs, but ensuring product quality.
